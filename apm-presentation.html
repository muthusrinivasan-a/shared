  • Deploy distributed tracing across all microservices

  • Deploy automated remediation workflows for common issues

  • Establish performance center of excellence
  • Scale practices across entire application portfolio

Application Health Scorecard Framework

Performance Rating Categories

OPTIMAL

Score: 90-100

GOOD

Score: 75-89

ACCEPTABLE

Score: 60-74

DEGRADED

Score: 40-59

CRITICAL

Score: 0-39

Scoring Dimensions (20 points each)

1. Availability & Reliability (20 pts)

  • 20 pts: Uptime ≥99.95%, Zero outages
  • 15 pts: Uptime ≥99.9%, ≤1 outage/month
  • 10 pts: Uptime ≥99.5%, ≤3 outages/month
  • 5 pts: Uptime ≥99%, ≤5 outages/month
  • 0 pts: Uptime <99%, >5 outages/month

2. Performance (20 pts)

  • 20 pts: P95 <200ms, Error rate <0.1%
  • 15 pts: P95 <500ms, Error rate <0.5%
  • 10 pts: P95 <1s, Error rate <1%
  • 5 pts: P95 <2s, Error rate <3%
  • 0 pts: P95 >2s, Error rate >3%

3. Security & Compliance (20 pts)

  • 20 pts: No vulnerabilities, 100% compliant
  • 15 pts: No critical vulns, >95% compliant
  • 10 pts: <3 high vulns, >90% compliant
  • 5 pts: <5 high vulns, >80% compliant
  • 0 pts: Critical vulns present, <80% compliant

4. Operational Excellence (20 pts)

  • 20 pts: MTTR <15min, Full automation
  • 15 pts: MTTR <30min, >80% automated
  • 10 pts: MTTR <1hr, >60% automated
  • 5 pts: MTTR <2hr, >40% automated
  • 0 pts: MTTR >2hr, <40% automated

5. Business Impact (20 pts)

  • 20 pts: Zero business impact, NPS >80
  • 15 pts: Minor impacts, NPS >70
  • 10 pts: Moderate impacts, NPS >60
  • 5 pts: Significant impacts, NPS >50
  • 0 pts: Severe impacts, NPS <50

Best Practices & Recommendations

Cultural Transformation

  • Foster a performance-first mindset across all development and operations teams
  • Implement blameless postmortem culture to encourage learning from failures
  • Encourage proactive monitoring and optimization vs reactive firefighting
  • Establish performance champions in each team for knowledge sharing

Technical Excellence

  • Standardize instrumentation across all applications using OpenTelemetry
